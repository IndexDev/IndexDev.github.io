  • Recovering password?

    If you have lost your password you can recover it with the help of your email. You do this by navigating to the recovery page, entering the email address you registered with and clicking the "Request Reset" button. Once you are done with the previously mentioned steps you will receive and email titled "TheAltening password reset" which contains a button called "Reset password", by clicking it you will be redirected to the password reset page, where you will be able to enter your new password. If you do not have access to your email you will have to contact the staff with the following information of your most recent payment: payment method and transaction id. You can contact our staff on discord, which you can join through the panel.

  • How can I use my own accounts again?

    Just run the Authenticator and click the dropdown. After that you'll be able to select "Mojang" and it will switch you to mojang's authentication servers.

  • How do I favorite or private an account?

    On TheAltening's website, navigate to the generator page. If you generate a few alts you will notice there is several icons including a lock and a heart, if you click on an account's Heart it will favorite the account, and if you click the lock it will make that alt private for you. You can private up to 5 alts.

  • How to use TheAltening?

    You can use our accounts very easily once you have downloaded the Authenticator. This program is necessary to load accounts from TheAltening. Once you have downloaded the authenticator, open it and select "TheAltening" then Navigate to "Generator" on TheAltening's website and click generate. You will recieve a token above the generator button; copy this token and paste it into your minecraft launcher, no password is needed and then you can login with it.

  • MineBot

  • How to link the bot to the altening?

    1. Select The Altening option under the Account path (Next to Linear, Random)
    2. Get your API Token from the account page.
    3. Enter it in the appropriate field and hit enter.
    4. Close the altening window.
    5. Click Start on the bot

  • How to use the bot with the altening?

    After you link your bot to TheAltening, simply change the join mode to TheAltening, it is under account path and next to linear/random modes.

  • What is "pick type" option and how to use only favorite/private accounts.

    Using the "Pick Type" option you can select what kind, and what method accounts are used in TheAltening. You can select private/favorite under the pick type option and it will only use those accounts.

  • Favorite/Private accounts from minebot.

    You can "favorite" and "Private" accounts from TheAltening directly from OQMineBot by first connecting the alt, and then in the "Accounts" tab of OQMineBot you right click on the alt and click the desired menu option.
